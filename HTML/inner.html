About Us

Who we are :

We at Godwin Construction Co., Would like to introduce ourselves as one of the CIVIL ENGINEERING CONTRACTORS and LAND DEVELOPERS in the City of Chennai.

Our humble beginning began in the year 1995 in the name and style as Godwin Construction Co., Civil consultant & Contractors. When we look back in the past years we have been given opportunities to carry out number of Civil Construction like Schools, Hospitals, Churches, Bungalows and Residential Buildings through leading architects like Mr. I. Venkateshwara Loo Vijayawada, Mr. Antony Bindu Chennai. Mr. Philip New Delhi, M/s. Kingsway Consultancy Chennai, Mr. Souriraj Chennai and have won whole hearted commendation in this regard. We are always working hard to shape the clients dreams to reality and the achievement of our goals.

While Godwin Construction utilizes state of the art technology in all steps leading up to the execution of a project, it is in the various projects where our commitment to state of the art technology realy shines. This commitment returns dividends not only in providing cost effective solution but also in speed of production, which saves you time and money. This is accomplished by equipping our range of equipment with the latest in construction industry.
